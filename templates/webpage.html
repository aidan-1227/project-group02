GOATED GROWTH

Group Analysis By:

FAMILY TREE

Enter list of dam tags to compare

To find the growth rate of all the children of any dam/s:

  1. Enter 1 or many tags for the dams you want to compare. To enter multiple dams, separate each tag by a comma
  2. Hit the 'submit family tree' button

For example: 123, 456, 789

BIRTH COHORT

Enter the earliest birthdate of a birth cohort

Enter the latest birthdate of a birth cohort

To find the growth rate of all goats in a given birth cohort:

  1. Click the first calendar icon and select the earliest birth date, or type it out manually
  2. Click the second calendar icon and select the latest birth date, or type it out manually
  3. Hit the 'submit birth cohort' button

Ensure that the earliest date is before the latest date

ALL GOATS

Select a group to analyze





To find the growth rate of all goats in one of the above groups:

  1. Select the desired group by clicking on one of the above buttons
  2. Hit the 'submit all goats' button

For further explanation, 'Is dead' will only analyze all the goats marked as dead, 'Is alive' will only analyze all the goats that are not marked as dead and are not marked as sold, 'Is sold' will analyze all goats that are marked as sold, and 'All goats' will just analyze all goats growth without any markers.
